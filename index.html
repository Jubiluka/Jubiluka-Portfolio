About Me

Hello! I'm Julia Moraes, a Brazilian-American Computer Science and Game Design student at DigiPen.

I love video games and animation, and I’m especially passionate about building the systems and mechanics that bring them to life. While I enjoy gameplay programming, I also have strong experience in systems programming and level design, which allows me to approach problems from multiple angles.

What makes me unique is my blend of technical and creative experience. With a background in programming and design, along with a personal passion for art, I’m able to think about games holistically — from core mechanics to UI to overall player experience. I love taking ideas and turning them into something interactive and meaningful.

When I approach a problem, I tend to think of it like a puzzle. I enjoy exploring different solutions and refining them until they feel both effective and elegant. At the same time, I always try to keep the player experience in mind, making sure that what I build is not just functional, but intuitive and engaging.

I care deeply about integrity, transparency, and collaboration. I believe every idea deserves to be heard, even if it doesn’t make it into the final product, and I value working in environments where people feel comfortable contributing and experimenting.

I'm currently seeking opportunities in game development where I can contribute to meaningful player experiences and continue growing as a developer.

Professional Experience
